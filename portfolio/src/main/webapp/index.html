About Me

College

I am a rising junior at Santa Clara University majoring in Computer Science and Engineering. As a campus ambassador for Google, I organized events on campus, introducing fellow students to Google products.

Google Cloud Platform Student Innovator

As the first Google Cloud Platform Student Innovator at my university, I hosted Google Cloud Platform workshops, tabled at annual hackathons as a mentor, and gave class presentations to undergraduate and graduate students.

Google Developer Student Club Lead

As a Google Developer Student Club Lead, I was invited to attend Developer Student Clubs United States Leads Summit 2019.

High School

I was born and raised in the Bay Area, and I attended Palo Alto High School. In high school, I founded two local clubs, Coding Trek and Silicon Valley Star Trek Club, and I was featured in four news articles.

Coding Trek

As the founder of Coding Trek, I designed a computer science curriculum for beginners and organized a series of free coding workshops for all ages. My club was featured in Los Altos Town Crier.

Silicon Valley Star Trek Club

To Boldly Go Where No One Has Gone Before

As the founder of Silicon Valley Star Trek Club, I organized weekly discussions about artificial intelligence, utopia, diversity, equality, justice, humanity, and technology. I also collected and compiled 300 Star Trek quotes. My club was featured in The Mercury News, Palo Alto Online, and Los Altos Town Crier.
